The single-cell dataset used in this work is stored in "single_cell_data_set.excel", which is 
derived from reference:
"Guo, G. ,Huss, M., Tong, G. Q.,Wang, C., Li Sun, L., Clarke, N. D. and Robson, P. (2010)
Resolution of Cell Fate Decisions Revealed by Single-Cell Gene Expression Analysis from Zygote
to Blastocyst. Development Cell, 18, 675-685".
